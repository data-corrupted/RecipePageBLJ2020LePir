How to bake butter cookies

Step 1: Preparation

First, you'll have to prepare every ingredient. You need:
  • 250g butter
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 300g flour
  • a pinch of salt
  • 50g flour to roll out the dough
  • Kitchen stuff like bowls
Cut the butter into pieces and wait for it to get soft.
Yes, I use the metric system. The imperial system is a MESS.

Step 2: Mix the butter and the sugar

As soon as the butter is soft enough, mix it with the powdered sugar.
I'm using a fork because my mixer would instantly break if I tried to use it.
After mixing, it should look like this.

Step 3: Adding flour

Now that we mixed the butter and the sugar we're going to add the flour.
I'd recommend to add it small steps, so it's easier to mix.
Also, use your hands to knead the dough. I definitely didn't try to knead it with a fork.

Step 4: Into the refrigerator

If your dough looks like the one in the picture, then you'll want to put it into the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

Step 5: make the cookies!

Before you start with this, remember to preheat your oven on 180Â°C. I definitely didn't forget to do that.
Now, get your cookie-making-thingies and get creative! Or be boring like me and use the same shape for every single cookie. It's the taste that counts! Use flour to roll out the dough. Trust me, it's worth it.

Step 6: into the oven

Now your cookies are ready for the oven. Wait about 10 minutes.
They are ready if they look golden and have thin brown borders.

Step 7: don't burn them

Woo they are finished! The only thing left to do is to wait for them to cool down.
